An English translation of 《香港出師表》 (archived version) by 畫字 (Draword).

六月廿一，行動升級，不撤不散。
Twenty-first of June: action escalates. No withdrawal: no dispersion.
行動在即，寫左篇出師表。雖係劣改，不過一字一句，都係我一直以黎嘅經歷同諗法。到時見。
Action is nigh. I have written a Memorial unto the Mobilisation. Though it be a poor edit of the original, yet each word and each sentence are from my experiences and thoughts all along. See you all then.
《香港出師表》
"Memorial unto the Mobilisation, Hong Kong"
民陣創業未半，而中道式微。今林鄭企硬，煲底疲弊，此誠危急存亡之秋也。
The Civil Front's undertaking is not yet half complete, and waneth admist the path. Today Lam Cheng stands firm, and those at the Pot's Bottom are weary: this is truly a period of critical urgency, and life and death.
  • 秋： period; lit. autumn; or year
然泛民議員，不懈於內；香港市民，遊行於外者，蓋追烈士之遺志，欲報之於我城也。
The pan-democrat councillors fight without slack on the inside. The people of Hong Kong, those rallying on the outside, do recall the determination bequeathed of our martyr, and wish to repay him in our City.
  • 烈士： our martyr
    梁凌杰 (Leung Ling-kit), who fell from atop Pacific Place on 15 June 2019, and died.
誠宜三罷上街，以光義士遺德，恢弘志士之氣。不宜妄自菲薄，互斥其非，以塞抗爭之路也。
We truly ought to join the Three Strikes and go upon the streets, to illuminate the virtue bequeathed of our righteous fighter, and to enlarge the spirit of our determined fighter; we ought not belittle ourselves recklessly and reproach each other's wrongs, to block the road of the struggle.
  • 菲，上聲，Cantonese: fei2, Mandarin: fěi
勇武和理，俱為一體；守護香港，不宜異同。
Whether courageous or peaceful and rational: all be united in one entity. In protecting Hong Kong: there ought not be disagreement.
若有篤灰互隊，及為踢爆者，宜付臉書，論其刑賞，以昭網民平明之理；不宜私怨，使內外不和也。
If there be ratting out and mutual dobbing, and those making exposition thereof: we ought to submit these to facebook, to debate the punishments and rewards due of them, to highlight the fair and impartial judgement of the netizens; we ought not hold personal grievances, and make ourselves discordant within and without.
  • 隊： dobbing; lit. poking; or fingering; or shoving
    篤 is also "poking".
  • 論： debate; or judge
  • 理： judgement; lit. governance
連登、高登巴打、絲打，此皆良實，各出奇謀，是以網民簡拔以為實用。
The bruthas and sistas of LIHKG and HKGolden: these are good and honest folk, each suggesting remarkable tactics; these are selected by the netizens to be actually employed.
愚以為送中之事，事無大小，悉以咨之，然後施行，必能裨補闕漏，有所廣益。
I the fool think that on issues of the China Extradition, no matter the issue be great or small: all should be consulted of them, and only afterwards be put into action, that we can surely supplement and mend the gaps and flaws, and that there be a broadening of benefit.
  • 裨：助也，補也； supplement; or aid
    裨， Cantonese: pei1, Mandarin: bì
  • 闕
    Used for 缺.
  • 漏： flaws; or loopholes
議員鄺囝，性行勤懇，曉暢調停，大眾稱之曰神，是以眾議舉鄺為和事。
Councillor Kwong the kiddo: his character and actions are diligent and earnest, and he understandeth and is versed in mediation; the great masses refer unto him as a deity, and by this, the masses moved to recommend Kwong as mediator.
  • Removed colon before 神.
  • 闕
    Used for 缺.
  • 和事： mediator; lit. harmoniser of matters
愚以為，議會之事，事無大小，悉以咨之，冀能使議會內外，連成一線也。
I the fool think, that on issues of the Council, no matter the issue be great or small: all should be consulted of them, in the hope that we can make those within and without the Council be connected in one political line.
親人民，遠專制，此港英所以興隆也；
親專制，遠人民，此港共所以傾頹也。
Being close unto the people and keeping far from the dictatorship: this was that by which British Hong Kong arose in prosperity.
Being close unto the dictatorship and keeping far from the people: this is that by which Communist-ruled Hong Kong shall collapse in ruin.
  • 遠： keeping far from
    遠，去聲， Cantonese: yün6, Mandarin: yuàn
先父在時，每與我論此事，未嘗不歎息痛恨於老董、狼英也。
When my late father still was, whenever discussing these matters with me, not once did he not sigh in abhorrence upon old Tung, and upon Ying the wolf.
文宣、物資、前線、參謀，此悉忠良死節之位，願大家信之任之，則反送中之隆，可計日而待也。
Disseminating, supplying, manning the front line, and picketing: these are all positions of loyalty and goodness, upheld unto death; I hope everyone does keep faith in them and trust them, that the prosperity of having opposed the China Extradition, may come within numbered days.
  • 待： come; lit. be awaited upon
我本廢青，躬耕於屯門，苟且偷生於亂世，不求聞達於社會。
I originally was a useless youngster, my person ploughing the fields of Tuen Mun, negligent and keeping a covert, plebeian life in an age of unrest, not seeking to be heard of or known in society.
中共不以港兩制，肆意染紅，再三推行惡法於市民之中。
But the Chinese Communists do not according to the Two Systems of Hong Kong, brazen in intent to dye us Red, and have again and again pushed Evil Laws upon the midst of the people.
  • 再三： again and again; lit. again and thrice
    By my count at least thrice: Article 23 (2003), Fake universal suffrage (2014), and now Extradition to Mainlandia (2019). And don't forget the proposed National Anthem Bill, whereby one might be arrested for mentioning 通菜牛肉剁爛.
由是痛恨，遂許泛民以驅馳。
From these abhorrences, I then agreed to the pan-democrats to do service for the cause.
  • 驅馳： do service for the cause; lit. run quickly (by horse)
後值傘運，受打於催淚警棍，佔領於夏愨龍和，爾來四年有多矣。
Afterwards I went through the Umbrella Movement, and was smote by tear-inducing gas and police baton, and did occupy in Harcourt and Lung Wo Roads; how over four years have come since then.
  • Replaced 𢡱 U+22871 with 愨 U+6128.
烈士知民善忘，故臨崩寄全體市民以大事也。
Our martyr knew that the people were good at forgetting; therefore, just before his downfall, entrusted he the whole body of the people with those important matters, that they be reminded.
受命以來，夙夜憂歎，恐託付不效，以傷義士之明。
Since receiving my mission, I have worried and sighed day and night, fearing that that entrusted in me hath not been effected, and harmed the splendour of our righteous fighter.
  • 效： effected; or brought about
故二十設限，要求回應。
Therefore, a deadline was established for the twentieth; we sought a response from the authorities.
今港共無賴，抗爭勢弱，當上街三罷，行動升級，攘除奸凶，興復政府，還我香港。
Today the Communist Hong Kong government is unscrupulous, and the tide of struggle weak, and we should go upon the streets and join the Three Strikes; action shall be escalated; we shall remove the evil perpetrators, restore good government, and return us Hong Kong.
此我所以報義士，而忠香港之應分也。
This is that by which I shall repay our righteous fighter, and it is my allotted duty of loyalty to Hong Kong.
  • 應分： allotted duty
    Originally written 應份. 分 below also originally written 份.
至於斟酌損益，臨場應變，則巴打、議員、抗爭者之分也。
As for weighing up harm and benefit, and the appropriate responses in the moment: these are the allotted duties of the bruthas, the councillors and the demonstrators.
  • 斟酌： weighing up; lit. pouring the correct amount
願港人自發以行動升級之效，不效，則治己之責，以告烈士之靈。
I hope Hong-kongers of their own accord do escalate action for an effect; and be there no effect, then sanction me, by the blame rightfully due upon mine own self, to inform the spirit of our martyr.
若無興德之言，則隨教徒之聲， Sing Hallelujah to the LORD。
And if ye lack words which encourage virtue, then follow the sounds of the followers of the Christian religion: Sing Hallelujah to the LORD.
  • Sing Hallelujah to the LORD
    Originally written in lower case.
大家亦宜自謀，以臨場執生，堅持到底，深追義士遺志。我不勝受恩感激。
We all should also plan for ourselves, improvise in the moment, and persist unto the end, and recall deeply the determination bequeathed of our righteous fighter. I could not completely bear your kindness and properly give thanks.
  • 底： the end; lit. the bottom
  • 勝： completely
    勝，平聲， Cantonese: shing1 (post-merger: sing1), Mandarin: shēng1 (Nominally-Communist 統讀: shèng)
六月廿一，行動升級，不撤不散。
Twenty-first of June: action escalates. No withdrawal: no dispersion.
