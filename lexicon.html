  1. I am not a Literary Chinese expert. This lexicon is merely a means of improving consistency in my translations. Undoubtedly I will misinterpret words, but hopefully I will now do so consistently.
  2. This lexicon is not a dictionary. The listings of meanings, [parts of speech], and pronunciations are not complete, nor could they ever be.
  3. Pronunciation listings by 陰陽平上去入 are based on the classical tone classes, which generally accord with modern Cantonese. When they do not make sense in modern Mandarin, it is due to either (1) loss of entering tone, or (2) Government-regulated 統讀-readings in Mainlandia.
  4. 音-homophones are based on modern Cantonese.
  5. Non-literal renderings are marked by met. (for "metaphorical").
