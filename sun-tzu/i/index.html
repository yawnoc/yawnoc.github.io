Translation (links to paragraphs)

The splitting of each volume into paragraphs is an entirely modern practice, and the headings I have allocated are not part of the original text.

  • ¶1. Units of length (or distance)
  • ¶2. Units of weight (or mass)
  • ¶3. Units of capacity
  • ¶4. Names of large numbers
  • ¶5. \pi \approx 3, \sqrt{2} \approx 7/5
  • ¶6. Densities of metals etc.
  • ¶7. Rod calculus: vertical & horizontal forms
  • ¶8. Rod calculus: multiplication algorithm
  • ¶9. Rod calculus: division algorithm
  • ¶10. Volume conversion rates for grain & rice
  • ¶11. Some equivalent fractions (1)
  • ¶12. Some equivalent fractions (2)
  • ¶13. Rod calculus multiplication: 81 \times 81
  • ¶14. Rod calculus division: 6561 \div 9
  • ¶15. Results of multiplication & division: multiples of 9

  • ¶17. Results of multiplication & division: multiples of 7
