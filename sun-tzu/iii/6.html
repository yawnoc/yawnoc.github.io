《孫子算經卷下》 "Sun Tzŭ's Computational Classic: Volume III"
§6. Division utilising a rate

↑ Volume III
← §5
§6

This section gives a word problem where division by a rate is used to determine an amount.

Translation

Chinese source text: Version A, Version B, Version C, Version D.
Unless noted otherwise, I follow the text from Version D, 《知不足齋叢書》本.

今有租九萬八千七百六十二斛、欲以一車載五十斛。問用車幾何。
Suppose there be land-tax nine myriad, eight thousand, seven hundred [and] sixty-two barrels, [and we] wish to use one chariot [to] carry fifty barrels. [We] ask, how many chariots useth [it]?
答曰、一千九百七十五乘奇一十二斛。
Answer saith: one thousand, nine hundred [and] seventy-five chariots remainder twelve barrels.
術曰、置租九萬八千七百六十二斛為實、以一車所載五十斛為法。
Method saith: put [down the] land-tax nine myriad, eight thousand, seven hundred [and] sixty-two barrels as [the] dividend, [and] use that carried of one chariot, [even] fifty barrels, as [the] divisor.
實如法、即得。
[Taking the] dividend as [per the] divisor, [we] are done.
